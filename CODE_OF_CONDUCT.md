# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) (v2.1). In brief:

- Be respectful, inclusive, and patient.
- Assume good intent; give constructive feedback.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

Enforcement:
- Report incidents to the maintainers at maintainer@example.com.
- Maintainers will review and respond promptly; actions may include warnings or removal from the project.

By participating, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
